

# Pazuri Cafe — Landing Page Plan

## Overview
A premium, single-page landing page for Pazuri Cafe, a Tanzanian specialty coffee shop. The design will use a forest green (#2D5A27) and cream (#FDFBF7) color palette with Playfair Display headings and Inter body text for a boutique, welcoming feel.

---

## Sections (Top to Bottom)

### 1. Sticky Navigation Bar
- Logo text "Pazuri Cafe" on the left
- Links: Menu, About Us, Location, Socials
- Transparent on top, becomes solid cream/green on scroll
- Mobile hamburger menu

### 2. Hero Section
- Full-screen image slider (3-4 slides) with café/coffee imagery using placeholder images
- Semi-transparent forest green overlay on all slides
- Large centered "Pazuri Cafe" title in Playfair Display (white)
- Tagline: e.g., *"Tanzanian Specialty Coffee, Beautifully Crafted"*
- Two side-by-side CTA buttons (stack on mobile):
  - **"Reserve a Table"** — white outline button
  - **"Order Now"** — solid green button

### 3. Menu Highlights Section
- Cream background with a few featured items displayed as cards (coffee, pastries, etc.)
- A prominent "Download Full Menu (PDF)" card with a clipboard icon
- Clean grid layout

### 4. About Us Section
- Two-column layout: engaging copy on the left explaining "Pazuri" (Beautiful) and the café's Tanzanian roots; atmospheric image on the right
- Warm, story-driven tone

### 5. Location & Contact Section
- Google Maps embed placeholder (Masaki/Oysterbay, Dar es Salaam)
- Contact details: +255 phone number, email, physical address
- Business hours: Mon–Sun, 7:00 AM – 10:00 PM
- Two-column layout (map + details)

### 6. Footer
- Social icons: Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp (Lucide icons)
- "© 2024 Pazuri Cafe — Made in Tanzania 🇹🇿"
- Quick nav links

---

## Design & Technical Details

- **Color Palette:** Forest Green (#2D5A27), Cream (#FDFBF7), Gold/White accents
- **Fonts:** Playfair Display (headings), Inter (body) — imported via Google Fonts
- **Icons:** Lucide React
- **Fully responsive** with mobile-first approach
- **SEO:** Semantic HTML5 tags, meta description/keywords placeholders targeting "Best Coffee in Dar es Salaam," "Pazuri Cafe Menu," "Tanzanian Specialty Coffee"
- **No backend needed** — purely static landing page
